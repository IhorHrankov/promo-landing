QA Leadership Scope

The non-technical surface area of the role — owning quality end-to-end, shaping process, and growing people.

I.

Quality Ownership

  • Own and enforce quality gates end-to-end across the platform
  • Establish, track, and report on quality metrics
  • Lead defect triage and drive defect lifecycle management
  • Review manual and automated test cases for coverage and quality
  • Own test planning for release iterations
II.

Process & Standards

  • Continuously improve QA processes in line with industry best practices
  • Maintain and evolve process documentation
  • Conduct risk assessments and escalate quality concerns to stakeholders
  • Implement and roll out Test Case Management Systems (Xray, TestRail, QTest)
III.

Cross-team Collaboration

  • Build and maintain strong working relationships with Product and Engineering teams
  • Act as the primary QA point of contact for cross-functional initiatives
IV.

Team Planning & Delivery

  • Define team priorities and manage workload distribution
  • Provide work estimation for QA efforts across projects
  • Assign tasks and oversee execution to ensure timely delivery
V.

People Management

  • Conduct regular 1-2-1s to support team growth and wellbeing
  • Lead onboarding for new team members
  • Carry out annual and mid-year performance reviews
  • Participate in interviewing and hiring QA candidates
